Game Rules:

Movement:

Press:

  • W: To Jump
  • A: To Move Left
  • D: To Move Right

Placing Blocks, TNTs and Stun Grenades:

Drag and Drop as required.

Shooting:

The Gun Points in the direction of your mouse

Left click to shoot. The gun has to reload before you could shoot again.

Zombies:

The Zombies will attack you and reduce your health

You must shoot them to kill them. They will break any blocks that come in their path.


Types of zombies:

  • Zombie 1:Killing this zombie gives you 5 points and 5 coins
  • Zombie 2: Beware! This zombie can pass through blocks! It has more health and deals more damage to the player
    Killing this zombie gives you 10 points and 9 coins
  • Zombie 3: Beware of this zombie! Its health regenerates in addition to having yet more health and dealing more damage.
    Killing this zombie gives you 20 points and 16 coins

Screen Look:

Guns:

Each gun has its own ups and downs

You may use 1,2,3 to choose between the guns (OR) simply click on the gun's icon

  • Semi-Automatic Rifle: Low reload time and average damage
  • Pistol: Average reload Time and above-average damage
  • Shotgun: High reload Time but High Damage

Power-Ups

There are several Power-Ups that can be bought using coins:

  • Faster Reload: Reduces reload time for all guns for 10 seconds
  • Temporary Immunity: Makes Player immune to any damage for 7.5 seconds
  • +50 Health: Increases player's health by 50 points
  • Jetpack: Allows the user to use a jetpack, this can be activated by pressing SpaceBar

TNTs and Stun Grenades

To use them:

  • First, place some TNTs and stun Grenades during preparation phase, before zombies arrive
  • Next, click on Use tnt or Use stun grenade, each requires 5 coins
  • Now, click on the TNT or Stun grenade you'd like to use, respectively before the time (10 seconds) runs out. Failing to do so would mean you wasted 5 coins.

Save your Game:

When you lose, that is the zombies kill you, a pop up will allow you to enter your name and save the game

Leaderboard:

There is a local leaderboard of all games played in your browser

This may be accessed by clicking on the "Leaderboard" button at the top of this page.

Leaderboard is based solely on number of points obtained in the game.
